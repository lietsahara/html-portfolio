Whispers of Spring

Cherry blossoms fall,
Whispers of springtime secrets,
Nature’s gentle dance.

Cherry blossoms fall,
Their delicate petals pirouette,
Caught in the breeze's tender embrace.

Beneath the blush-pink canopy,
Secrets unfold—
The ancient language of petals,
A love letter from the sakura.

Sunlight weaves through branches,
Painting dappled patterns on the ground,
As if the sky itself dances with joy.

And in this ephemeral moment,
Nature whispers its truth:
Life is fleeting, yet exquisite,
A fragile dance of seasons.

So let us linger here,
Beneath the cherry blossoms,
Where time slows, and beauty blooms,
And the heart finds solace in petals.
